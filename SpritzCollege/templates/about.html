Welcome to SpritzCollege

SpritzCollege is an institution that offers an integrated living and learning environment, with both hotel-like and academic services for university students. The facility combines room management, similar to a hotel, with the management of students' personal and academic data, facilitating the handling of additional activities such as enhancement courses, conferences, or events.

The features include overseeing various facilities like student rooms, study halls, and libraries, all digitalized to simplify bureaucratic and organizational aspects and enable the generation of customized statistical reports. SpritzCollege also manages enrollment fees and allows easy booking of facilities, in addition to tracking maintenance interventions and event participation.
